Rules and Regulations

Code of Conducts for Girl Students

1) The Girls students will have to follow all the rules laid down by the hostel committee.

2) The Girls has to be present for evening attendance.

3) Each Girl has to sign on attendance.

4) Each Girl will have permission to visit town/home twice in a month (Except in case of emergency).

5) Each Girl will fill up a form and seek permission from the Rector while leaving the premises.

6) Male will not be allowed inside the hostel.

7) Visitors meeting time is up to 6:00 pm.

8) Female relative would be permitted to stay in hostel with permission by warden and shall be liable to pay the charge.

9) In case of Medical issues, girls should communicate to the Rector.

10) Girls will be liable to penalty for any damage of hostel property singly or collectively.

11)Complaints regarding electrical/carpentry/plumbing jobs should write in the form of application.

12)Use of unauthorized electrical appliances such as rod, kettle, induction stove and heater etc. are strictly prohibited.

13)Room Furniture, electrical fittings are required to be maintained by students in good condition.

14)Electric equipment must be switched off while leaving rooms.

15)The room of any girls in hostel can be inspected at any time by Rector.

16)Modification or addition to Hostel Rules may be made from time to time which shall be communicated as and when necessary.
