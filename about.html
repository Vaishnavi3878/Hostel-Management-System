ABOUT US

There is a separate provision of hostel for ladies. The Women's Hostel established in the year 2007-08. The college has very good hostel accommodation for girls. 21 Rooms are
available in the hostel for residential purpose. The ladies hostel building located at the north of the campus. The most of the girls from the disadvantaged community, seeking admission, are accommodated. Several girls from rural areas prefer to seek admission to our college because of the excellent hostel facility on the college campus.
